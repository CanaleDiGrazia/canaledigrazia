About Canale Di Grazia

Canale Di Grazia Incorporated combines formulation science with disciplined manufacturing to deliver reliable products for brands. With an FDA license and a focus on evidence-based ingredients, we support partners with formulation, stability testing, regulatory readiness, and scalable production.

Our approach is intentionally faceless — we center the product, the process, and the outcome, not personalities. We believe in manufacturing that is consistent, compliant, and built for long-term brand success.
